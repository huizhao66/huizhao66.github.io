Brief Bio

               
Dr. Zhao is an Assistant Professor at University of North Texas in the Computer Science and Engineering department. She is the PI of Lab for  Emerging Computer Architectures (LARC).
Her research interests are multi-core and GPU accelerated computing systems, including Network-on-Chips, memory systmes and heterogeneous computing. She is currently investigating hardware accelerator designs for Deep Neural Networks, including GPUs and FPGAs.
She received her BS and MSEE degree from Harbin Institute of Technology and Northeastern University respectively. She received her Ph.D degree from the Pennsylvania State University.
